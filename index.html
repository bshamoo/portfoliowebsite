    ABOUT

    Hi! I’m Brandon Shamoo. Welcome to my website.

    I’m currently an incoming 3rd-year Computer Science and Engineering student at the University of California, Irvine. My area of study focuses on the collaboration between computer and software systems engineering to promote the development of innovative systems and services.

    Most recently, I have had the opportunity to participate in AT&T’s Summer Learning Academy Externship in June and July. During this time, I acquired business and technical acumen along with personal growth and professional development in leadership, communication, technology, and more. I have also had the opportunity to identify my top strengths through the CliftonStrengths® assessment and look forward to applying them to my future work. Here are my top 5 CliftonStrengths®: Learner, Developer, Restorative, Empathy, and Individual.

    Outside of school and work, my passions include spending time with friends and family, playing and creating video games, listening to music and curating playlists, watching and playing sports, and traveling to exciting locations to experience new cultures.

    One of my goals for Summer 2020 is to develop and establish my personal brand. To achieve this, I decided to create a portfolio website where I can showcase myself through my passions, work, skills, and accomplishments. This website stands as a project reflecting my curiosity to learn new skills and determination for self-improvement. To read more about my development process for this website, please visit the PROJECTS module in the work section.

    If you find a bug or have a design recommendation, just let me know. Any feedback is much appreciated.

    Thanks for visiting. Have a great day!
